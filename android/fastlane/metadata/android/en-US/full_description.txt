Pluvio is a free, open-source rain radar for Belgium and the Low Countries.

What it does
• Animated radar from the past hour through the next two hours.
• Per-location precipitation nowcast: see when rain starts, how intense it'll be, and when it'll stop.
• Brussels, Antwerp, Liège, Gent, Charleroi — anywhere with KMI radar coverage.

Privacy first
• No ads, no trackers, no account.
• Location stays on your device.
• Data comes from KMI / IRM open data (opendata.meteo.be) under CC BY 4.0.

Made in Belgium, by Appmire.
Source code: https://github.com/appmire/pluvio
